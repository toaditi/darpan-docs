## SFTP

Open **Ask Darpan** and search for `SFTP`, `file server`, the host, username, or `edit SFTP`. Check host, port, username, authentication method, directory path, and network access. Confirm whether the server expects a private key, password, or another authentication method.

## NetSuite

Open **Ask Darpan** and search for `NetSuite`, `credentials`, `token`, `OAuth`, `RESTlet`, or `endpoint`. Check account target, role permissions, token validity, integration status, script deployment, and SuiteQL field names.

## AI providers

Open **Ask Darpan** and search for `AI`, `API key`, `model settings`, `OpenAI`, or `Gemini`. Check the provider record, credential reference, model availability, and feature-specific limits.

## Safe debugging

Capture error codes and messages, but redact secrets and customer-specific identifiers before sharing externally.
